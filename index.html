ABOUT THE BOOK

Meet Danny, a little girl who is skeptical about her first school dance. Join Danny on her fun journey with her friends Sherry and Freddie. Children will like reading, rhyming and looking at the pictures illustrated by someone close to their age. A perfect book on self-esteem the whole family can read and enjoy together.

The author, Navika, teamed up with her 8 year old niece Niya after discovering her passion for creative drawings and sketches. Navika was looking for an illustrator to work with her who could connect to her readers through the book's illustrations. Niya was thrilled to do the illustrations for the book and it was a fun project for them to work on together..

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Navika is an Indo-Canadian born and raised in Canada’s capital. She is a debut author of her first book of the “Danny’s First” series. She loves to read books with her two children which lead her to write a book for her kids, nieces and nephews. She works in the Human Resources field and writes in her leisure time. She is the wife of a rockstar engineer and lives in Ottawa, Ontario.
